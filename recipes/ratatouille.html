Ratatouille

Description

Ingredient (4 serving)

  • 4 tomatoes
  • 2 courgettes
  • 2 aubergines
  • red pepper
  • green pepper
  • 2 onions
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 5cl of olive oil
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cut each of your vegetables in cube
  2. Saute each of your vegetables with olive oil and put them in a pot
  3. Simmer for 30 to 40 min
